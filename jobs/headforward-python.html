Overview

Python/Golang Developer: £30-£50K per anum We’re an outsource development company based about 5 miles from the beach in Cornwall. We build dedicated teams for our clients, so there’s no sitting on the bench or swapping around. We’ve recently reached 100 employees but work in small teams so have the combination of a start-up feel with the security of a larger company. Our culture is pretty relaxed and work-life balance is important to us.

People are our number 1 priority and everything we do is to ensure that our teams are happy. We work and live in an amazing location which we make the most of whenever we can.
