Overview

CrowdStrike is looking to hire a Senior Cloud Engineer to build large scale cloud-based cybersecurity systems using Golang, Python, Cassandra, Elasticsearch, AWS. We’re looking for a highly-technical, hands-on engineer who loves to operate at scale and build globally distributed, fault tolerant and highly scalable cloud-based critical systems.

We are the fastest growing endpoint protection company, one of the World’s Most 50 Innovative Companies according to MIT, and one of Forbes Most Promising Companies. Our growth and innovation are driven by incredible employees who deliver unmatched customer success.

At CrowdStrike we operate a massive cloud platform that protects our customers from a variety of bad actors: cyber criminals, hacktivism and state sponsored attackers. We process tens of billions of events a day and we store and use petabytes of data.
